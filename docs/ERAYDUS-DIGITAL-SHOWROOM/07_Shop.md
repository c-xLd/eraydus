# Purpose

The Collection page is not a traditional ecommerce product listing.

It is a premium digital gallery where customers discover, compare and personalize shower cabin solutions.

Visitors should feel like walking through an architectural showroom rather than browsing an online store.

The page should prioritize inspiration before purchase.

---

# Design Philosophy

No small product cards.

No crowded grids.

No discount stickers.

No marketplace feeling.

No unnecessary banners.

Everything should be elegant, spacious and premium.

Every product should have room to breathe.
