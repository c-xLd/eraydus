1. PRODUCT VISION
Mission

Erayduş will not become another shower cabin ecommerce website.

Erayduş will become the most premium digital showroom experience in the bathroom industry.

Visitors should not feel like they are browsing products.

They should feel like they are walking through an architectural showroom where every interaction reflects craftsmanship, engineering, precision and luxury.

The website must increase trust before price.

It must increase desire before sales.

It must educate before converting.

Vision Statement

Create the world's most immersive digital platform for luxury shower cabins by combining world-class industrial design, architectural visualization, interactive product customization and enterprise-grade customer experience.

The website should be capable of competing visually with premium brands such as Apple, Tesla, Porsche Design and Rimadesio while remaining highly optimized for SEO, performance and lead generation.

Core Philosophy

The website is NOT an ecommerce website.

The website is a Digital Experience Platform.

Every page must tell a story.

Every interaction should increase confidence.

Every animation should reinforce premium quality.

Every transition should communicate precision.

Brand Position

ERAYDUŞ is positioned as a premium manufacturer of custom shower cabin systems rather than a seller of standard products.

The platform should emphasize:

• Custom craftsmanship

• Architectural quality

• Precision engineering

• Premium materials

• Modern design

• Long-lasting durability

• Personalized production

Customer Experience Goal

When visitors enter the website they should experience the following emotional journey.

First 5 Seconds

"Wow."

The visitor immediately realizes this is different from every other shower cabin company.

First Minute

"I trust this company."

Trust is established through elegant design, transparency, certifications, completed projects, and detailed product information.

During Configuration

"This product is being designed specifically for me."

The configurator should feel like designing a luxury car rather than purchasing a standard bathroom product.

Before Contact

"I already know what I want."

The website should answer every major question before the customer asks.

After Contact

"I made the right decision."

The WhatsApp ordering experience should feel seamless and premium.
