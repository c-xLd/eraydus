# Brand Philosophy

ERAYDUŞ is not a shower cabin seller.

ERAYDUŞ is a premium architectural bathroom brand that designs custom shower experiences.

The website should communicate craftsmanship, engineering excellence, elegance and trust before focusing on price.

Every screen must reinforce quality.

Visitors should never feel they are browsing a catalogue.

They should feel they are visiting a luxury showroom.

---

# Brand Personality

## Elegant

Minimal.
Confident.
Timeless.

Never loud.

Never cheap.

Never crowded.

---

## Precision

Every line.

Every animation.

Every spacing.

Every interaction.

Everything should communicate precision engineering.

---

## Architectural

Design should feel inspired by architecture instead of ecommerce.

Use large photography.

Large whitespace.

Perfect alignment.

Strong typography.

Minimal interface.

---

## Premium

Luxury without showing luxury.

Expensive without looking expensive.

Modern without chasing trends.
