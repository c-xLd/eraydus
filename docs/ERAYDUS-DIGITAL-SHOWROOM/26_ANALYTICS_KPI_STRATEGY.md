# PURPOSE

This document defines the analytics, measurement and business intelligence strategy for the ERAYDUŞ Digital Experience Platform.

Every visitor interaction should generate meaningful insights.

Analytics exists to improve customer experience, increase conversions and support data-driven decision making.

Never collect data without a business purpose.

---

# BUSINESS OBJECTIVES

Increase qualified quote requests.

Increase configurator completion rate.

Increase organic traffic.

Increase returning visitors.

Increase average session duration.

Reduce bounce rate.

Improve conversion rate.

Increase project portfolio engagement.

Strengthen brand awareness.

---

# MEASUREMENT PRINCIPLES

Measure only what creates value.

Respect user privacy.

Use first-party analytics whenever possible.

Validate all tracking before production.

Maintain consistent event naming.

Document every tracked event.
