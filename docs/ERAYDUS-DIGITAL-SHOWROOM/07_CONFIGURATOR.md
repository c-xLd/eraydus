# OVERVIEW

The Configurator is the heart of the entire platform.

It is not a form.

It is not a calculator.

It is a premium product customization experience inspired by:

- Porsche Car Configurator
- Tesla Design Studio
- Apple Product Builder
- IKEA Planner
- Bevel Shower Configurator

The goal is to make users feel they are designing a luxury product made specifically for them.

---

# USER EXPERIENCE

The configurator must be distraction-free.

No advertisements.

No popups.

No unnecessary text.

Focus entirely on the product.

Every change must be visible instantly.

Every selection should increase confidence.
